SECURITIES AND FUTURES ACT
(CHAPTER 289)
SECURITIES AND FUTURES (EXEMPTION OF
OUE HOSPITALITY REAL ESTATE INVESTMENT TRUST)
REGULATIONS 2019
ARRANGEMENT OF REGULATIONS
Regulation
1.
Citation and commencement
2.
Exemption
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 337(1) of the
Securities and Futures Act, the Monetary Authority of Singapore
makes the following Regulations:
Citation and commencement
1. These Regulations are the Securities and Futures (Exemption of
OUE Hospitality Real Estate Investment Trust) Regulations 2019 and
come into operation on 18 September 2019.
Exemption
2. The responsible person for OUE Hospitality Real Estate
Investment
Trust
is
exempt
from
the
requirement
under
section 295(2) of the Act to take the necessary steps to wind up
OUE Hospitality Real Estate Investment Trust following the
withdrawal of the Authority’s authorisation of OUE Hospitality
Real Estate Investment Trust under section 288(7) of the Act.
