Chapter 559
Trade Marks Ordinance
10/12/2020
Introductory
10.
Registered trade mark a property right
(1)
A registered trade mark is a property right obtained by the
registration of the trade mark under this Ordinance.
(2)
The owner of a registered trade mark has the rights and is
entitled to the remedies provided by this Ordinance.
(3)
No proceedings lie to prevent, or to recover damages for, the
infringement of an unregistered trade mark but nothing in this
Ordinance affects the law relating to passing off.
