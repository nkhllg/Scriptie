In exercise of the powers conferred by section 13(12) of the Income
Tax Act, the Minister for Finance makes the following Order:
Citation
1. This Order is the Income Tax (Suntec Real Estate Investment
Trust — Section 13(12) Exemption) Order 2021.
Exemption
2.—(1) Trust distribution income described in sub-paragraph (2),
that is received in Singapore on or after 18 June 2021 by the trustee of
Suntec Real Estate Investment Trust or Suntec REIT UK 1 Pte. Ltd. (a
Singapore-incorporated company), from Victoria Circle Unit Trust 1
(a Jersey property unit trust) or Victoria Circle Unit Trust 2 (a Jersey
property unit trust), is exempt from tax.
(2) Sub-paragraph (1) applies to the following income:
(a) any rental or other property-related income derived from a
specified property;
(b) any gain from the disposal of a specified property;
