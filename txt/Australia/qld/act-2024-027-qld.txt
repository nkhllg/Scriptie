29
Procedures for selling goods by auction—Act, 
s 363
(1)
This section prescribes procedures under section
363(5) of the Act for a person selling goods by
auction under section 363(4)(a) of the Act.
(2)
The person must make reasonable efforts to
contact the person entitled to the goods (the
owner) to give the owner notice of the auction. 
(3)
For subsection (2), reasonable efforts include the
following—
(a)
attempting to contact the owner by
telephone, including text message, email or
private message on a social media platform;
(b)
attempting to contact an emergency contact
listed on the owner’s residential tenancy
agreement;
(c)
publishing a notice in an online newspaper
for the city or State in which the owner is or
was residing.
