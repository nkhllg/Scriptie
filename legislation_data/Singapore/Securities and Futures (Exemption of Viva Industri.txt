SECURITIES AND FUTURES ACT
(CHAPTER 289)
SECURITIES AND FUTURES (EXEMPTION OF
VIVA INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE INVESTMENT TRUST)
REGULATIONS 2019
ARRANGEMENT OF REGULATIONS
Regulation
1.
Citation and commencement
2.
Exemption
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 337(1) of the
Securities and Futures Act, the Monetary Authority of Singapore
makes the following Regulations:
Citation and commencement
1. These Regulations are the Securities and Futures (Exemption of
Viva Industrial Real Estate Investment Trust) Regulations 2019 and
are deemed to have come into operation on 23 October 2018.
Exemption
2. The
responsible
person
for
Viva
Industrial
Real
Estate
Investment
Trust
is
exempted
from
the
requirement
under
section 295(2) of the Act to take the necessary steps to wind up
Viva
Industrial
Real
Estate
Investment
Trust
following
the
withdrawal of the Authority’s authorisation of Viva Industrial Real
Estate Investment Trust under section 288(7) of the Act.
