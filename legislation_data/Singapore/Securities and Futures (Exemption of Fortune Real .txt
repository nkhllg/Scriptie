SECURITIES AND FUTURES ACT
(CHAPTER 289)
SECURITIES AND FUTURES (EXEMPTION OF
FORTUNE REAL ESTATE INVESTMENT TRUST)
REGULATIONS 2019
ARRANGEMENT OF REGULATIONS
Regulation
1.
Citation and commencement
2.
Exemption
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 337(1) of the
Securities and Futures Act, the Monetary Authority of Singapore
makes the following Regulations:
Citation and commencement
1. These Regulations are the Securities and Futures (Exemption of
Fortune Real Estate Investment Trust) Regulations 2019 and come
into operation on 22 October 2019.
Exemption
2. The responsible person for Fortune Real Estate Investment Trust
is exempted from the requirement under section 295(2) of the Act to
take the necessary steps to wind up Fortune Real Estate Investment
Trust following the withdrawal of the Authority’s authorisation of
Fortune Real Estate Investment Trust under section 288(7) of the Act.
